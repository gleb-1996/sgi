About Us

Stroygazengineering Limited Liability Company was established in 2013. Initially, our company specialized in performing a full range of construction and installation works at gas industry facilities.

LLC "Stroygasengineering" is a member of the self-regulatory organization Association "Association of General Contractors in Construction".

Stroygazingineering LLC has certificates of compliance with the requirements of GOST R ISO 9001-2015, GOST R ISO 14001-2016.

From 2013 to 2021, the total volume of construction and installation work performed by our enterprise at the technological infrastructure facilities of Gazprom Group companies and EuroChem JSC MCC exceeded 1.3 billion rubles.

Recently, our company has focused its efforts on a new direction of activity - the supply of electrical products of domestic production for the needs of oil and gas industry enterprises.

In 2022, Stroygazingineering LLC became a distributor of TSN-Electro LLC products (Nizhny Novgorod) in the Russian Federation and the countries of the Commonwealth of Independent States.

Today, like ten years ago, our company remains focused on systematic cooperation with its partners and customers.
