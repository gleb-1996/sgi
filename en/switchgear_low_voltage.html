LOW VOLTAGE SWITCHGEAR (RUNN)

Low-voltage complete devices (NKU) are intended for use as part of power supply, control and automation systems as switchboards, power distribution points, control and automation panels and cabinets, and can be used as switchgear from the low voltage side of complete transformer substations.
